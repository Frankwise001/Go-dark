What is this

THE BRIEF

Why a Shadow Corps?

GoDark is building the first real institutional dark pool in crypto — off-chain execution with on-chain settlement, full privacy, no front-running. Shadow Corps exists to make sure every serious trader, DeFi protocol, and institutional desk knows that — not through noise, but through credibility and consistency.

One Team. No Tiers.

Shadow Corps is a flat structure. All 50 ambassadors carry the same title, the same access, and the same expectations. Performance separates people — not arbitrary levels. The top 10 performers get rewarded biweekly. The most outstanding one each month earns the Affiliate Badge. Everyone else stays hungry.

Who This Is For

  • Crypto Twitter / Farcaster voices with institutional or DeFi audiences
  • Community managers inside trading-focused DAOs or funds
  • Content creators covering privacy, MEV, or institutional crypto
  • KOLs in Discord trading communities
  • Builders, analysts, and educators in the dark pool / DeFi space

What Makes This Different

No bot farms. No engagement bait. No tier politics. Shadow Corps ambassadors earn by delivering real growth signals — quality content that gets traction, organic referrals, and education that actually changes how people think about dark pools in crypto. The leaderboard doesn't lie.

What Ambassadors Do

AMBASSADOR
RESPONSIBILITIES

2–3
Weekly Content Minimum
Ambassadors are required to publish a minimum of 2 to 3 quality pieces of content per week — across any platform (X/Twitter, Farcaster, YouTube, TikTok, etc.). This is the non-negotiable baseline. Falling below it for two consecutive weeks triggers a formal review.

Content

CREATE

  • Minimum 2–3 quality original GoDark-related content pieces per week — tweets, threads, short videos, or long-form posts
  • Content must be original and add your own voice — not copy-pasted from GoDark announcements
  • Educational content about dark pools, institutional crypto, and GoDark's product is prioritized
  • Branded visuals encouraged — all assets provided in the content kit

Community

ENGAGE

  • Engage authentically with GoDark's official posts — meaningful replies, not just likes
  • Participate actively in Discord: ambassador chat, calls, and leaderboard discussions
  • Introduce at least 2 qualified contacts to GoDark per month via your referral link
  • Represent GoDark accurately — no false claims, no FUD, no speculation presented as fact

Reporting

REPORT

  • Submit monthly content report — links to all content, referral count, and audience feedback notes
  • Flag product questions or community confusion you're observing — this feeds directly into GoDark team insights
  • Attend at least 1 monthly ambassador call on Discord (mandatory; absence is logged)

Standards

UPHOLD

  • Maintain professionalism across all public-facing GoDark-related posts
  • Do not simultaneously represent a direct GoDark competitor without prior disclosure
  • Bring at least 1 institutional or high-value referral per quarter — fund, protocol, or trading desk
  • Stay current on GoDark product updates — ambassadors must know what they represent

Program Direction

HOW I
RUN THIS

Lead Ambassador (Maryjane)

Vet & Onboard

I vet every applicant and onboard the 50 ambassador slots. I decide who gets in and whether they're the right fit for Shadow Corps.

Activate

I run monthly content briefs, monthly Discord calls, and keep the group informed, energized, and moving.

Evaluate

I track the leaderboard, run 90-day reviews, and make the final call on Affiliate Badge selection each month.

Report

I deliver monthly program reports to GoDark leadership — metrics, highlights, community sentiment, and strategic recommendations.

Monthly Rhythm

  • Week 1 — Drop monthly content brief in Discord: key messages, upcoming announcements, content prompts, and graphic assets
  • Week 2 — Biweekly leaderboard locked; top 10 rewarded. Mid-month Discord update with engagement challenge or bonus task
  • Week 3 — Check-in thread in Discord ambassador channel. Shoutout top performers; privately flag anyone falling behind
  • Week 4 — Full point tally. Monthly leaderboard posted publicly in Discord. Affiliate Badge selected and announced. Payouts triggered

My Tools

  • Notion or Airtable — Master ambassador tracker: name, points, content links, referrals, review status
  • Discord — Primary hub for everything: ambassador chat, leaderboard, monthly calls, announcements, content drops, and badge announcements
  • Google Form — Monthly self-reporting form (content links, referral counts, audience feedback)
  • Typeform or Tally — Public application form for new cohort intake
  • Content Vault — Shared Google Drive with branded graphics, approved copy, and templates

Keeping Ambassadors Engaged

  • Biweekly top 10 reward creates a recurring two-week motivation cycle — not just a monthly sprint
  • Affiliate Badge adds a prestige layer that points alone can't replicate
  • Monthly content prompts and challenges prevent creator block and give clear direction
  • Public Discord leaderboard creates healthy competition without toxicity
  • Fast feedback: acknowledge great content within 24h — retweet, reply, signal boost
  • Surprise bonus drops for standout moments keep the energy unpredictable and exciting

Handling Inactive Ambassadors

  • Week 1–2 below minimum — private Discord DM; offer support, content ideas, or context check-in
  • Week 3–4 still below minimum — formal warning issued; slot placed at risk
  • Month 2 without recovery — off-boarded respectfully; slot immediately opened for waitlist
  • All 50 slots run on rolling performance windows — no permanent seats for inactivity
  • A public waitlist is maintained so vacant slots are filled quickly

Growing the Program

  • Start at 50 — fill slots strategically across geography, niche, and audience type
  • Ambassadors can nominate trusted candidates — referral-driven intake maintains quality
  • Public application windows each quarter create buzz and FOMO
  • Track which ambassadors drive institutional referrals — double down on those profiles
  • At 6 months, evaluate whether to expand to 75–100 based on program ROI and GoDark's growth stage

Reporting to GoDark

  • Monthly summary: total content pieces, impression estimates, referrals generated, points distributed, and standout wins
  • Affiliate Badge rationale — brief note on why the selected ambassador stood out that month
  • Community sentiment report: what people are asking, what's confusing, what's resonating
  • Quarterly strategic review: program health, cost per referral, ROI, and next-quarter recommendations
